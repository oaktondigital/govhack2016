About

Harness the power of virtual reality to explore Canberra’s beautiful bushland and local attractions. Learn about Australian native animals through a fun, interactive and engaging app. Simply turn on your location, start walking and discover a world of exciting creatures.

Features

Using data sets from the Atlas of Living Australia this app uses recorded instances of wildlife around you while walking along one of the identified tourist trails in the ACT, such as the Centenary trail.

Interact with information specific to your location, as well as add your own photographic data to share with other community members using the app.

Use augmented reality to interact with local wildlife. Whether you are a local or a visitor to Canberra this app gives you a unique introduction to life and the natural biodiversity that surrounds the bush capital.
